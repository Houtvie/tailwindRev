About us

Welcome to the

Department of Software Engineering

Reliable solutions through software engineering. Emphasizing systematic, practical, and quantifiable approach to the development, operation, and maintenance of software systems..

The Department of Software Engineering at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO) is part of the School of Information and Communication Technology (SICT), which was established on August 30, 2018. The department itself began admitting students during the 2019/2020 academic session, making it one of the newer additions to the SICT alongside the Department of Cyber Security. Below is a comprehensive overview of the Department of Software Engineering at FUTO based on available information.

The Department of Software Engineering at FUTO focuses on addressing the global demand for software applications across critical sectors such as transportation, finance, health, education, and social networking. It emphasizes a systematic, practical, and quantifiable approach to the development, operation, and maintenance of software systems. The program is designed to produce graduates who are innovative, skilled, and capable of creating high-quality software systems within constraints of time, budget, and other requirements.
